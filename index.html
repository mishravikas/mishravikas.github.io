I'm a PhD Student in the Spirals team at Inria Lille since October 2018. My supervisors are Walter Rudametkin, Romain Rouvoy and Pierre Laperdrix. I also help in developing amiunique.org

My research interests include Security & Privacy with a special intrest in Privacy issues in online tracking.

Besides research, I also enjoy contributing to open source projects and have previously contributed to Mozilla and OSGeo among other organizations.
